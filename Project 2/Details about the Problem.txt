COVID-19 and its impact on education, social life and mental health of students: 

In this study, a cross-sectional survey is conducted with a sample size of 1182 students of different age groups 
from different educational institutions in Delhi National Capital Region (NCR).

The client has shared some requirements to be developed - 

1. Dashboard Overview
Create a dashboard showing key insights:
Average time spent on online classes, self-study, and fitness.
Most preferred social media platform.
Average rating of online class experience.

2. Age-wise Analysis
Develop a report showing:
Age group distribution.
Time spent on online classes and self-study by age group.
Health issues during lockdown segmented by age.

3. Geographical Insights
Create a map visualization:
Highlight the region-wise average time spent on fitness, social media, and sleep.
Show the region-wise rating of online class experience.

4. Correlation Exploration
Build scatter plots to analyze:
Correlation between time spent on fitness and change in weight.
Correlation between time spent on sleep and health issues.

5. Family Connection Analysis
Create a report showing:
Percentage of respondents who feel more connected to family and close friends.
Breakdown of this metric by region and age group.

6. Stress Busters Analysis
Create a bar chart to:
Display the most common stress busters during the lockdown.
Show their popularity across different age groups.

7. Time Utilization Overview
Develop a stacked column chart:
Show the average distribution of time among online classes, self-study, fitness, social media, and TV.

8. Social Media Preferences
Build a pie chart or bar chart:
Highlight the preferred social media platforms.
Compare time spent on social media by platform.

9. Health and Lifestyle Changes
Create a report showing:
Number of meals per day vs. change in weight.
Proportion of respondents who experienced health issues during lockdown.

10. What People Miss the Most
Build a word cloud or bar chart:
Display the most missed activities or things during the lockdown.
Segment the results by age group and region.

The dashaboard should be clean with proper formatting and color.
